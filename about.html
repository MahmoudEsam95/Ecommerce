About Sheltero

It is an integrated Egyptian real estate market on the Internet. It provides you with a digital experience service, whether to buy or sell real estate, with a comprehensive service that makes it easy for you to achieve the deal anywhere in Egypt, whether it is an owner or a buyer, by showing the appropriate areas for you on the map that provides you with all the details about the property.

Our Mission

We organize and facilitate your dealings in the real estate market by using the website instead of the traditional methods.

Our vision

To be the main driver for developing and enhancing confidence in the Egyptian real estate market.
